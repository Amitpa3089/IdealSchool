Air Cooled Building

Ours is the first air cooled building in the city. The fresh air cooling and feeling is attained by the installation of several number of air coolers which help in keeping the atmosphere light & fresh.The campus is facilitated with 30 air cooling machines which keep the temperature down and provide fresh air. During the designing of our air cooled campus we keep in our mind that the throw of air cooling machines is towards the face of the students
