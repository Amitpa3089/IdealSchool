Personality Development Classes

We are focusing on providing quality education to children of our school but the students seem to lack confidence when it comes to public speaking, interaction and spoken English. These special classes are a step towards personality development of our students. Various types of activities are organised in these special classes including classes on general knowledge, moral values, spoken English and competitions like speech, debate, extempore and much more which help students to get rid of stage fear and fear of public speaking, making them more confident.
