Infirmary Room

Ideal International School has an Infirmary Room (Medical Room) to provide medical care to the students. To boost the feeling of wellness in every unwell child. The room is named as Wellbeing Room. A regular in-house General Physician helps the school in providing quality primary health care. The room is well-equipped with all the medical facilities. School also ensures that the first aid kits are kept at different areas in the school for any kind of emergency. The first aid equipments, supplies and facilities are kept clean, dry, and are ready to use. It is accessible at any time whenever and wherever required. The First Aid kits are placed at the front desk, front gate, back gate, laboratories and in school buses to care of any injury or illness.
