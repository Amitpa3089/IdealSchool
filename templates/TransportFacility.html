Transport Facility

Ensuring safe transportation is our first priority as services to the parents with assurance as per the entire required guidelines . A fleet of buses provides the facility of commuting between home and school to those who wish to avail it. The buses ply on fixed routes and stop at fixed places only. Factors taken into account while deciding the routes and the bus stops are: space availability for U-turn instead of moving the bus in reverse direction, safe stop over a place where students can board and alight comfortably without any risk, number of students from a particular area, convenient to maximum number of students. And, since the institute is located in the city therefore least travel time is one big aspect for our students as well as the parents.
