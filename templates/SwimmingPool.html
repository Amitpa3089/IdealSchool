Swimming Pool

Water activities are termed as best of all activities which provide complete exercise to whole body. Ideal International campus is facilitated with the indoor swimming pool along with filter plant. The size of our swimming pool is 82 x 27 feet i.e. as per the Olympic norms.

The most obvious benefit of having an indoor pool is that it will provide with an all-weather recreation area. This can definitely provide better swimming experience throughout the year. This means maximising the use of swimming pool for our students.

Another advantage of having an indoor pool is not exposed to outside debris and dust that is normally blown into an outdoor pool. Because of this, it ensures the clean water for swimming of students and no harm from UV rays of sunlight and its extreme heat.
